# Shadow of the Shogun — Ninja Infiltration

A cinematic **2D ninja infiltration platformer** built with **pure HTML5 Canvas, CSS, and JavaScript**. Sneak through a moonlit Japanese castle, avoid deadly traps, defeat guards, collect sacred scrolls, and challenge the Shogun’s elite captain in the throne room.

---

## 🎮 Game Overview

**Shadow of the Shogun** is a side-scrolling action platformer inspired by classic ninja and samurai games. The player controls a lone shinobi infiltrating a heavily guarded castle at night.

### Objective

Travel through five unique zones:

* **The Moonlit Garden**
* **The Crimson Gateway**
* **The Stone Lobby**
* **The Tatami Hall**
* **The Shogun’s Throne Room**

Collect scrolls, survive traps, defeat enemies, and reach the final chamber to complete the infiltration.

## 🧠 Gameplay Systems

### Combat

* **Katana:** Short-range, high-damage slash.
* **Shuriken:** Fast projectile for distant enemies.

### Enemies

* **Guards:** Patrol between waypoints and attack on contact.
* **Archers:** Fire arrows when the player enters range.
* **Boss:** Uses charge attacks and increased damage.

### Survival

Falling into pits or taking too much damage costs a life. Checkpoints allow respawning without restarting the entire game.
